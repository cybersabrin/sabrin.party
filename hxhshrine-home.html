so what is hunter x hunter?

Hunter X Hunter (or hxh) is a manga series written by the man the myth the legend Yoshihiro Togashi. he started writing the series in 1998 and the story is still ongoing! the manga was adapted into an anime series in 1999 and then again in 2011. as i show u my favorite characters you'll see images from both versions, though i like the 1999 art style more... ☆⌒(ゝ。∂)

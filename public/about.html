hello i'm sabrin aka weenie. i'm everybody's favorite twenty something year old indecisive unemployed guy who spends way too much time online. i don't talk about my politics on here as much as i should, but i am a leftist and you will probably see that shine thru in some of the things i talk about, especially when it's about art. i believe art is never apolitical and i am very interested in radical and transgressive art in general.

i use this site as a little home for me to pour all my love for the things i care about in one place. a junk journal of sorts. i mostly use this to update my media log, but you'll also find some digital shrines and collections here and there.
